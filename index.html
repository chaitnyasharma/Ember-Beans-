More than coffee, it's a little ritual.

We roast in small batches, play records all day, and know our regulars by name. Whether you're crashing deadlines or catching up with friends, Ember & Bean was built to feel like your living room—just with better espresso.

Every cup starts with responsibly sourced beans from small farms we know and trust. Our baristas dial in the espresso throughout the day so your first sip is never an experiment—it's a promise.

Hungry? Our pastry case is baked in-house every morning: flaky croissants, chewy cookies, and a dangerously good cinnamon roll. Vegan and gluten-friendly options rotate weekly.
